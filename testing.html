FCC Registration Number (FRN)

It is highly recommended that you get an FRN number before attending a testing session. It can take the place of a Social Security Number and in some cases, it will be the only number you can use. Remember to write it down or store it someplace safe after you receive it. You will need to bring it with you to testing sessions.
